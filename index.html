FAQ

Can orgs meet during the week of Career Fair or the week of the reading period?

No.

Can my org invite representatives from companies outside of the CAP list?

Yes, but contact Alyshia Jackson and/or Troy Peace beforehand.

Do I need to order pizza?

For weekly meetings, no; Alyshia Jackson will order pizza for you. For other events, you will have to inform Alyshia Jackson about the food that you need. On the day of your event, the pizza will be ordered and delivered for you.
